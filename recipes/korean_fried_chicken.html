Korean Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

This is the BEST KOREAN FRIED CHICKEN. Crispy fried chicken drenched in a spicy gochujan sauce. This is not complicated whatsoever, and the result would not disapoint you!

Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

  • 4 Chiken Breasts
  • 1 Cup (240ml) of Buttermilk

Fot the Gochujang Sauce

  • Gochujang (obviously)
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spring Onions

Steps

  1. First we marinade the chicken in buttermilk, salt, white pepper and garlic salt.
  2. Then we coat the chicken in my special secret mixture. Only joking, it's not secret. Find the full mixture in the recipe card below.
